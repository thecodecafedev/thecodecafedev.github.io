The Code Café

About Us

Our mission at The Code Café is to introduce school students to the world of coding. As a team of college students majoring in computer science, we understand the challenges faced by young aspiring coders in starting their coding journey. This is why we at The Code Café are dedicated to providing free virtual coding workshops.

The Advantage

Coding at a young age is essential because just like learning a language, a child's brain is quick to pick up and integrate these skills. It is never too late to start coding, but kids who start early have a massive advantage. Learning these skills early on helps build the brain's natural tendency for identifying and applying logic, and provides a wider base for future learning.

The Thought

Students are taught history, not all go on to become historians, they are taught economics, not all go on to become economists. Students are taught a variety of different subjects to let them figure out for themselves what they’re interested in.

The Goal

Introducing Coding would definitely spark interest in the subject among students, because they would finally get to use all those ideas that sit in the back of their heads the whole day, and get a break from the regular classes. And one day they could be the founder of a major tech company.

The Motto

Do it for you, Do it for your community, Do it to make the world a better place.
