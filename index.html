Mission

Our mission at The Code Café is to introduce school students *across India?* to the world of coding. As a team of college students majoring in computer science, we understand the challenges faced by young aspiring coders in starting their coding journey. This is why we at The Code Café are dedicated to providing free virtual coding workshops.
